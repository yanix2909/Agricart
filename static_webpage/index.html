Connecting Farmers to Consumers

AgriCart is a revolutionary smart agriculture platform designed to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers in Ormoc City, providing fresh, quality agricultural products directly from farm to table.

About Cabintan Livelihood Community Agriculture – Cooperative (CALCOA)

Location: Purok 3, Barangay Cabintan, Ormoc City

Established: 2005

Members: 50 active farmer members growing quality vegetables

Activities: Vegetable production under protective and open-field systems, agricultural supply distribution, and retail services for institutional buyers

Our Purpose

AgriCart addresses the critical challenges faced by farmers in accessing wider markets and consumers in obtaining fresh, locally-grown produce. By leveraging modern technology, we eliminate middlemen, reduce post-harvest losses, ensure fair pricing for farmers, and provide consumers with direct access to farm-fresh vegetables. Our platform empowers the agricultural community while promoting sustainable farming practices and supporting local food security in Ormoc City.

Smart Agriculture Innovation

CALCOA's innovative farming methods align with emerging smart agriculture trends, integrating technology-driven solutions to enhance productivity and sustainability. Through AgriCart, we're bringing these innovations directly to consumers, creating a transparent and efficient supply chain that benefits both farmers and the community.

Impact on the Community

By supporting local farmers through AgriCart, consumers contribute to:

  • Sustainable livelihoods for 50 farming families
  • Reduced food miles and environmental impact
  • Access to fresher, more nutritious produce
  • Fair prices for both farmers and consumers
  • Strengthened local food security

Rider Roles & Responsibilities

Order Management

View and accept orders assigned by CALCOA staff. Track all assigned deliveries in real-time.

Delivery Execution

Navigate to pickup and delivery locations. Update order status at each stage of delivery.

Customer Communication

Maintain professional communication with customers. Ensure timely and quality service delivery.

App Features

Unified Order Interface

Single dashboard displaying all orders - both out for delivery and your assigned orders. Filter orders by status (All, Out for Delivery, Assigned, Picked Up, In Transit). Real-time updates when staff assigns new orders.

Direct Order Assignment

No acceptance step required - start working immediately when staff assigns orders to you. Orders appear instantly when staff marks them as "Out for Delivery" and assigns them to you.

Status-Based Actions

Assigned Status: "Mark as Picked Up" button to confirm product collection.
Picked Up Status: "Start Delivery" button when heading to customer.
In Transit Status: "Complete Delivery" button upon successful delivery.

Real-Time Synchronization

All status updates sync instantly between rider app and staff dashboard. Staff can monitor your delivery progress in real-time. Automatic updates ensure everyone stays informed.

Comprehensive Order Details

View complete order information including customer name, phone number, and delivery address. See order items, quantities, and total amount. Access delivery instructions and special notes from customers.

Location Information

Access detailed customer addresses with sitio, barangay, city, and province information. View landmarks and special delivery instructions. Optional map links for easy navigation.

Smart Order Filtering

Filter orders by status to focus on what matters. View "Out for Delivery" orders available for acceptance. Track your "Assigned", "Picked Up", and "In Transit" orders separately.

Delivery Dashboard

View your delivery statistics and performance metrics. Track completed deliveries and earnings. Access quick actions for common tasks.

Instant Notifications

Receive push notifications for new order assignments. Get alerts for important updates from staff. Stay connected throughout your delivery shift.

Profile & Settings

Manage your rider profile and contact information. Update your availability status. View your delivery history and performance ratings.

Secure Authentication

Secure login system for authorized riders only. Staff-managed rider accounts ensure platform security. Protected access to customer information.

Optimized for Mobile

Designed specifically for on-the-go delivery operations. Works smoothly on various Android devices. Minimal data usage for efficiency.

Delivery Workflow

  1. Order Assignment: Staff assigns order to you → Status: "Assigned"
  2. Product Pickup: Collect products from CALCOA → Tap "Mark as Picked Up"
  3. Start Delivery: Head to customer location → Tap "Start Delivery"
  4. Complete Delivery: Deliver to customer → Tap "Complete Delivery"
  5. Success: Order marked as delivered, customer can rate your service
